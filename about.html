About Me

Camryn Gillmor

I excel in telling stories with digital products.

A lifelong story teller, I started performing at a young age and devoted my formative years to perfecting my coloratura soprano and comedic timing (the trick is comedy comes in threes). Years spent collaborating in rehearsal rooms and musty theaters taught me how to listen and lead a team. It instilled in me a strong work ethic and a passion to never stop learning. As I transitioned into the professional world, I found a home in the event industry and new passion designing and creating digital products.

I have a wholistic approach to design, focusing first on empathizing with the user, researching and defining their needs. Then on the organization KPIs and the symbiotic relationship between both parties.

Collaboration is key to my creative process. I have a special ability to organize chaos into action- collaborating cross-departmentally to brainstorm new ideas, coordinate logistics, set timelines and execute action items.

As a leader, I thrive in high pressure environments and lead by example, not afraid to get in the trenches with my team. I bring a positive warmth to my work and am quick to lighten the mood with a little song or a goofy face during stressful times.

When I’m not working, I love to play with my daughter Katherine and husband Kevin, visit local museums and zoos, and belt out show tunes whenever possible.
